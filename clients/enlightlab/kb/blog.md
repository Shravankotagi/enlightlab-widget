## Blog Post — Why Fractional CTOs are the Secret Weapon for Series A Startups
- **URL Link**: https://enlightlab.com/blog/fractional-cto-startups
- **Summary**: Startups face a critical scaling bottleneck between product-market fit and building an engineering organization. A fractional CTO provides senior leadership (architecture, technical recruiting, and process alignment) at a fraction of the cost of a full-time executive. Key benefits: rapid architecture auditing, team mentoring, and scaling from MVP to production-readiness.

## Blog Post — Building Secure AI Agents: Mapping to HIPAA & SOC 2 Compliance
- **URL Link**: https://enlightlab.com/blog/secure-ai-agents-hipaa-soc2
- **Summary**: When deploying AI agents in healthcare or fintech, security is paramount. This post outlines how Enlight Lab designs data firewalls, scrubs Personally Identifiable Information (PII) before it hits LLM APIs, and enforces data access controls. All agents are designed to align with HIPAA and SOC 2 security principles to ensure compliance.

## Blog Post — Claude RAG Sidecars: Maximizing Grounding for Enterprise Apps
- **URL Link**: https://enlightlab.com/blog/claude-rag-sidecar-grounding
- **Summary**: Outlines the RAG "sidecar" architecture used by Enlight Lab to prevent LLM hallucinations. It details cosine similarity matching, semantic indexing using Gemini embeddings, and deploying strict negative constraints to force the LLM to refuse to answer rather than invent facts when a fact is out-of-context.
