# Process & Pricing Model

## Process — Assessment
**Step 1**: Discovery call, requirements gathering, technical feasibility review. Align on goals, constraints, and real-world conditions to build clear project foundation.

## Process — Solution Design
**Step 2**: Architecture outline, delivery roadmap, KPIs, risk assessment. Detailed proposal and project blueprint provided. Vision turns into structured plan.

## Process — Build Execution
**Step 3**: 2-week agile sprints, regular demos, consistent progress updates. Clear iterative cycles with quality at every step. Full visibility throughout.

## Process — Scaling & Optimization
**Step 4**: Performance tuning, resilience building, automation. Product fully prepared to scale with your growth.

## Process — Transition & Handover
**Step 5**: Full documentation, team training, operational guidance. Clean, confident transition. Your team takes over seamlessly from day one.

## Process — Collaboration
**Step 6**: Modern tools — Slack, Jira, Notion, GitHub. Streamlined communication, friction-free project management. Entire project stays connected.

## Pricing — General
Transparent pricing based on project scope, team composition, engagement type. No fixed prices published — every project is unique. Detailed proposals after initial discovery call.

## Pricing — Fixed Price
Best for well-defined projects with clear scope and deliverables. Know exactly what you're getting and what it costs upfront.

## Pricing — Time & Materials
Best for evolving projects where requirements may change. Pay for actual time and resources used, with full transparency.

## Pricing — Dedicated Team
Best for ongoing development needs. Dedicated team works exclusively on your product on a monthly retainer.

## Pricing — Fractional Leadership
CTO as a Service. Best for startups needing senior tech leadership without full-time hire. Flexible hours and commitment.

## Pricing — MVP Ballpark
Simple MVP typically starts at $15,000 to $40,000. Full-featured product ranges from $50,000 to $200,000+. Minimum project size around $10,000. Sweet spot: $25,000+ for significant value. Can start small with pilot project and expand.

## Pricing — Scope Changes
Structured change-control process for scope evolution. Clear impact assessments and full alignment before additional work. No surprise invoices.
