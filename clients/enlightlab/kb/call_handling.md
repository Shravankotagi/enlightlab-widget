## FAQ — Contact & Getting Started
Contact via contact form on enlightlab.com, email contact@enlightlab.com, or book a free discovery call. Team responds quickly with right technical guidance. Free 30-minute discovery call, clear proposal within 2-3 business days. NDAs signed before detailed discussions. Full IP rights transfer to client upon final payment.

## Call Handling — Service Inquiry
Ask what industry they're in and what problem they're trying to solve. Map to most relevant service. Offer to send case study or book discovery call.

## Call Handling — Pricing Questions
Explain transparent pricing model. Give ballpark ranges if pressed: MVP $15K-$40K, full product $50K+. Always recommend discovery call for accurate quote. Never quote fixed specific price without discovery call.

## Call Handling — Hiring Developers
Explain staff augmentation. Ask about role, tech stack, hours needed, timeline. Offer to connect with team for shortlist within 1-2 weeks.

## Call Handling — Complaint or Issue
Apologize for inconvenience. Take name and contact details. Assure team member will follow up within 24 hours. Escalate to contact@enlightlab.com.

## Call Handling — Unsure What They Need
Ask 3 qualifying questions: What industry? What problem are you trying to solve? Rough timeline and budget? Based on answers, recommend most relevant service and offer discovery call.

## Call Handling — Partnership Inquiry
Thank them for interest. Take name, company, email. Business development team will reach out within 2 business days. Forward to contact@enlightlab.com.

## Call Closing Options
- **Option A (Book discovery call)**: "I can have our team reach out to schedule a free 30-minute discovery call. Can I take your name and email?"
- **Option B (Send information)**: "I can have our team send you our service brochure and relevant case studies. What's the best email?"
- **Option C (Direct contact)**: "Reach the team directly at contact@enlightlab.com or visit enlightlab.com to fill out the contact form. They'll get back within 24 hours."
